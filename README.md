# Contemporary Sanskrit Observatory (CSO)

> A computational research infrastructure for documenting, preserving, and analysing contemporary Sanskrit communication.

The Contemporary Sanskrit Observatory (CSO) is an open research infrastructure dedicated to the systematic study of contemporary Sanskrit across spoken, written, audiovisual, and digital media.

Rather than treating texts as isolated objects, the Observatory models communication as a network of people, institutions, communicative events, artifacts, documents, and computational observations.

The project aims to provide a durable, extensible foundation for reproducible computational scholarship on modern Sanskrit while supporting comparison across genres, media, institutions, and communities of practice.
